NovaBuilder is a self-hosted platform for building and operating real business systems.

It focuses on long-term outcomes instead of short-lived tools and provides a modular foundation for creating, extending, and maintaining production-grade applications.

## What NovaBuilder is
- A system-first application platform
- Modular, extensible, and self-hosted
- Designed for real-world business and organizational use
- Built to evolve without vendor lock-in

## What NovaBuilder is not
- Not a website builder
- Not a typical no-code or low-code tool
- Not a SaaS product with hard platform limits

## Status
NovaBuilder is under active development.
The public release, documentation, and extension ecosystem are currently being prepared.
